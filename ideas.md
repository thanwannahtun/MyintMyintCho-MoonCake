# Selected Approach: Modern Heritage Fusion

I have selected the **Modern Heritage Fusion** approach. This style best balances the traditional nature of the Burmese mooncake with the modern "Coffee" flavor twist and the user's request for a "professional yet approachable" design. It allows for a warm, inviting color palette that suits a food product while providing a structure that can support the requested interactive charts and visualizations without feeling too clinical.

**Design Philosophy Statement**:
"We will create a digital experience that honors the rich tradition of Mandalay's mooncakes while celebrating the modern innovation of the coffee flavor. The design will be warm, inviting, and story-driven, using a palette of deep coffee browns and antique golds on a cream background. We will use asymmetric layouts and subtle traditional patterns to create a professional yet approachable aesthetic, ensuring the product feels both premium and familiar."
